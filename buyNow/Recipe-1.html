Chicken Curry Recipes

RecipeTin Eats Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • Chicken thighs
  • Garlic, ginger, onion
  • Curry powder
  • Coconut milk
  • Chicken stock
  • Frozen peas
  • Coriander

Instructions:

  1. Sauté garlic, ginger, and onion.
  2. Add chicken and cook until white.
  3. Stir in curry powder.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and chicken stock, simmer to thicken.
  5. Add peas and salt, cook briefly, and garnish with coriander.
  6. Serve over rice or noodles.
Source: RecipeTin Eats

Nicky's Kitchen Sanctuary Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Onion, garlic, ginger
  • Curry powder, ground coriander, cumin, paprika, cinnamon
  • Tomato puree, tinned tomatoes, chicken stock, coconut milk
  • Baby spinach
  • Fresh coriander, chili flakes for serving

Instructions:

  1. Sauté chicken and onions.
  2. Add spices and cook briefly.
  3. Stir in tomato puree, tinned tomatoes, stock, and coconut milk.
  4. Simmer until chicken is cooked.
  5. Add spinach and serve with rice, coriander, and chili flakes.
Source: Nicky's Kitchen Sanctuary

Swasthi's Recipes Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (bone-in preferred)
  • Onions, green chilies, ginger garlic paste
  • Tomatoes, yogurt
  • Turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder
  • Whole spices: bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom
  • Coriander leaves

Instructions:

  1. Sauté whole spices in oil.
  2. Add onions and chilies, cook until golden.
  3. Stir in ginger garlic paste, then tomatoes and spices.
  4. Add chicken and coriander leaves, cook until chicken turns white.
  5. Simmer with hot water until chicken is tender.
Source: Swasthi's Recipes

Wholesome Yum Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Onion, garlic
  • Diced tomatoes, coconut cream, chicken broth
  • Curry powder, ground ginger, paprika, sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions and brown the chicken.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.
  3. Simmer until chicken is cooked through and sauce thickens.
Source: Wholesome Yum
